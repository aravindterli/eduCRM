# CentraCRM Visual Flow Documentation

This document provides a visual guide to the core workflows within CentraCRM.

---

## 1. Dashboard & Analytics
The dashboard is the central hub for monitoring institutional performance. It provides real-time statistics on leads, admissions, and revenue, along with acquisition trends and a recent activity log.

![CentraCRM Dashboard](file:///C:/Users/aravi/.gemini/antigravity/brain/77dfef45-e1fd-4211-8ace-1de7054b6fa7/dashboard_flow_1773666225689.png)

---

## 2. Lead Management
The Lead Management page allows for efficient tracking and organization of admission leads. Users can search, filter, and add new leads manually or via bulk import.

![Lead Management List](file:///C:/Users/aravi/.gemini/antigravity/brain/77dfef45-e1fd-4211-8ace-1de7054b6fa7/leads_list_flow_1773666246517.png)

---

## 3. Lead Details & Quick Actions
Clicking on any lead opens a detailed side panel. This view provides quick access to communication shortcuts (Phone/Email), stage management, and a complete interaction history including webinar registrations and internal notes.

![Lead Details View](file:///C:/Users/aravi/.gemini/antigravity/brain/77dfef45-e1fd-4211-8ace-1de7054b6fa7/lead_details_flow_1773666259859.png)

---

## 4. Counseling Scheduler
The Counseling page is designed for counselors to manage their daily student interactions. It featuring an active counseling queue, a daily follow-up schedule, and a quick-notes section for task management.

![Counseling Scheduler](file:///C:/Users/aravi/.gemini/antigravity/brain/77dfef45-e1fd-4211-8ace-1de7054b6fa7/counseling_page_flow_1773666283609.png)

---

## 5. Marketing & Campaigns (Admin Only)
Administrators and Marketing teams can manage lead sources and marketing campaigns to track acquisition effectiveness and optimize outreach strategies.
*(Note: Access restricted based on Role)*

---

## 📱 Mobile Accessibility
The CentraCRM is fully responsive and can be installed as a PWA, providing floating action buttons (FAB) for quick lead entry while on the go.
