# EduCRM User Manual

Welcome to the EduCRM! This manual will guide you through the features and workflows of the system, tailored to your specific role.

---

## 🔑 Getting Started

### Login
- Access the CRM via the URL provided by your administrator.
- Enter your registered email and password to access your unique dashboard.

### Dashboard Overview
Your dashboard provides a high-level view of:
- **Metrics**: Total leads, new admissions, and monthly revenue.
- **Acquisition Trends**: Visualize where your students are coming from.
- **Recent Activity**: Stay updated on the latest system changes.

---

## 👥 Roles & Permissions

| Role | Access Level |
| :--- | :--- |
| **ADMIN** | Full control over users, settings, and all modules. |
| **MARKETING** | Manage Campaigns, Leads, Webinars, and view Reports. |
| **TELECALLER** | Capture Leads, schedule initial follow-ups. |
| **COUNSELOR** | Direct student counseling, managing applications, and conversions. |
| **FINANCE** | Manage fee structures, payments, and financial reports. |

---

## 📈 Lead Management

### Adding Leads
1. Navigate to the **Leads** tab.
2. Use the **Add Lead** button for manual entry or **Import** to upload a CSV file.
3. On mobile, use the floating **Quick Add** button (FAB) for instant capture.

### Engaging Leads
- Use the **WhatsApp** and **Call** icons in the lead list for immediate communication.
- Click on a lead to open the **Details Panel** where you can view interaction history and internal notes.

---

## 🎓 Counseling & Conversions

### Counseling Queue
- Access the **Counseling** tab to see your daily interaction list.
- Click "New Interaction" to log a counseling session.

### Scheduling Follow-ups
- In the Lead Details panel, click **Schedule Call** to set a reminder for a future date.
- Your scheduled calls will automatically appear in your **Counseling Dashboard**.

---

## 📱 Mobile Access (PWA)

EduCRM is a Progressive Web App. You can install it on your phone:
1. **Android**: Open the CRM in Chrome and tap "Add to Home Screen" when prompted.
2. **iOS**: Open in Safari, tap the Share icon, and select "Add to Home Screen".

---

## 🔒 Security & Privacy
- **Encryption**: Your sensitive student data is encrypted at rest.
- **Audit Logs**: All major actions are tracked for security and accountability.
- **Backup**: The system performs regular database backups to ensure data safety.
